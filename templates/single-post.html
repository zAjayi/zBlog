Changes

The concept of change is scary yet change is a constant. Getting off that plush couch and stepping out of our comfort zone is something no one ever wants to do. The thought of taking that first step into the unknown; like an explorer in an alien land, like a fine artist staring at a blank canvas armed with a paintbrush, like a writer gazing at a blank page, like a child taking its first step, it’s all so scary. The fear of falling the fear of failing the crippling terror that comes

with a blank mind… We tend to tell ourselves that change isn’t bad; that we’re probably overthinking it. At least, that’s what I tell myself; if only I could get a penny for each time I’d told myself to stop being dramatic. Almost like it was a sin to be afraid.It took me a while, but I realized that this fear is normal. This knot in my chest will not get tighter.

As much as we want to just crack open a soda and stuff our faces with chocolate cake while relaxing on a sofa and staring at a television screen, we can’t spend the rest of our lives like that. Life isn’t that easy. And despite how much we wish for it to be, life isn’t fair. It’s full of changes and like animals out of their natural habitat, we have to adapt. By all means, change is scary but it is inevitable. Change has to come in other for growth to happen. A bird is always scared to take its first flight but once it does, it never remembers that fear. Indeed, staying in our comfort zone seems like the safest option but I read somewhere that you actually need space to grow and that can only happen outside your safety bubble. Just like water takes the shape of whatever container it flows into, it is limited by it but an ocean has no limits; it flows into the land whenever it feels like it. So I urge you today, to take that first step. Even if you have to take a million first steps, do it!
